About us

+380962008523

Hello!
I am Mikhaylo Schaslyvyi, the founder of Forgemika.
With over twenty years of experience in the metalworking industry, I have witnessed the consistent growth and expansion of our capabilities.
Now, we create products that combine the strength of metal, the natural beauty of wood, and the unique appearance of resin – creations that reflect a modern perspective on craftsmanship.

The name "Forgemika" symbolizes transformation and innovation, signifying a long journey of development, skill, and a pursuit of new trends in the art of material processing. It merges two words: "Forge," meaning a smithy in English, and "Mika," my name creatively reimagined. This name represents the transition from traditional blacksmithing to new principles and contemporary trends. I invite you to discover Forgemika – a place where tradition, innovation, and the ability to transform ordinary materials into something special converge.

Our products

My goal is to be your guide in choosing interior solutions that not only transform your space but also inspire the creation of a unique style and cozy atmosphere in your home. Selecting furniture and decorative elements may seem like a routine task, but it doesn't exclude the opportunity to turn it into a creative and emotionally engaging process. I acknowledge that I have often received feedback about my excessive creativity in a seemingly ordinary field. However, this creativity helps in crafting extraordinary solutions. Let's identify the challenges that hinder making an optimal choice and explore possible ways to overcome them:

  • Challenges:

    Are you looking for unique, high-quality furniture and interior items that consider material quality, functionality, aesthetics, and affordability? Ones that reflect your style, are practical for daily use, customizable, and boast unique designs?

  • Obstacles:

    Choosing interior items is often complicated by market saturation with various options and brands, lack of product information, high prices for unique items, limited choices in local stores, doubts about quality, delivery issues, and a lack of personalized approach.

  • Solution:

    We offer customization of our products, provide detailed product information, a user-friendly online shopping interface, quality guarantees, after-sales services, publication of reviews and successful project case studies, and active support to our customers.

Our mission is to create unique, high-quality, author-designed interior products that not only showcase impeccable design and functionality but also embody harmony with nature. We strive to incorporate natural forms and materials into every product, infusing a touch of natural beauty and tranquility. Our aim is to inspire clients with our creations, creating a distinctive style and comfort in their homes while preserving and restoring forms that express our respect and love for nature.

Our product range includes:

Shoe benches: A perfect blend of style and functionality for your home, featuring a solid metal base for unique aesthetics and durable construction. The velvet-covered seat offers a comfortable space for storing favorite pairs of shoes, making it more than just a seating area – your personal comfort oasis in the foyer.

Biofireplaces: Crafted from select oak or pine wood, including "barnwood," these fireplaces exude sophistication and ecological consciousness. The vibrant and lively flames provide a sense of warmth and coziness to your space, running on bioethanol without emitting smoke, fumes, or soot.

Wall clocks: Author-designed clocks made from steel, wood, and epoxy resin, adding a unique charm to your space. Featuring plant compositions, smooth movement mechanisms, convenient and reliable wall mounting, they can also serve as unique gifts for any occasion.

Candleholders: Author-designed creations born from the combination of steel, wood, epoxy resin, or concrete, offering various variations.

Storage systems: An excellent way to efficiently and tastefully utilize space, including bedside tables, decorative shelves, bookshelves, clothing hangers, and more.

Wooden and epoxy resin lamps: More than just lighting, they are true works of art. Often reflecting the style of our clocks, they can be combined to illuminate your space with a new dimension of beauty. These lamps provide gentle, soft light, enhancing your interior even when turned off.

Tables and consoles: Every corner, every line – a symphony of materials and design. Created from exquisite wood species, from noble oak to the soft texture of pine, each piece of wood carries an intriguing story. The bold combination with metal and the transparency of epoxy resin creates aesthetics radiating modernity and elegance.

We offer both standard product options and individual solutions tailored to the specific needs and desires of our clients.
